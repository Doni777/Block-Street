Core Protocol

Built Different.
Executed On-Chain.

Block Street combines Wall Street execution standards with the transparency and programmability of DeFi.

⚡
Aqua RFQ Engine
Market makers compete via cryptographically signed request-for-quote model. Best-execution guaranteed on every trade with zero MEV leakage and institutional-grade latency.
🏔️
Everst Lending Protocol
Native lending, borrowing, and liquidation tooling built specifically for tokenized equities. Go long, short, or hedge — with DeFi-native risk controls.
🌐
LayerZero Multichain
Seamless cross-chain settlement via LayerZero. Deploy and trade across Ethereum, BNB Chain, and Monad from a unified liquidity layer with a single API.
📊
Tokenized Equities
Trade AAPL, TSLA, NVDA, MSTR and more on-chain. Not just wrapped tokens — fully borrowable, shortable, and hedgeable assets with real price discovery.
💎
USD1 / WLFI Integration
First dedicated USD1 launchpad. Providing USD1 liquidity infrastructure and stablecoin ecosystem tools aligned with the World Liberty Financial economy.
🔐
Institutional Security
Audited by Halborn Security. Quotes are cryptographically signed and verified on-chain to prevent manipulation. Enterprise-grade compliance baked in.
